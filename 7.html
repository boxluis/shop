Why Manpower?

Manpower offers you the opportunity to work with a wide variety of businesses, from start-up enterprises to Fortune 500 companies. You will be given the chance to try these work environments to learn what you like best. We offer Temporary to Full Time Employment, Human Resource Solutions, Direct Hire, Short Term, and Payroll Services for clerical, unskilled and skilled industrial, mining, technical, professional, production, trades, logistics/distribution, and contact center placements. And we have nine offices serving the Reno, Sparks, Stead, Fernley, Fallon, Carson City, Winnemucca, and Elko areas.

We go above and beyond typical job placement. Manpower Northern Nevada is committed to helping our employees build a valuable portfolio of skills and diverse work experiences. We have a comprehensive range of assessments that quickly enable us to focus on your skills, abilities, and knowledge. If you’re interested in learning new skills, Manpower offers hundreds of training courses and we will help you put your new skills to work right away.

What Makes Us Different

40 Years of Experience

We are celebrating 40 years of service in Northern Nevada. Our experienced team of over 30 is dedicated to providing high-quality human resource and staffing solutions.

Local Staffing & Payroll

Our administrative and payroll office is here in Reno, allowing a local touch. Checks and invoices are cut locally, not mailed in from out of state. This gives us the ability to correct any discrepancies in less than 24 hours. We offer direct deposit, Global Cash Card, and paper checks.

Urban level Service in Rural Nevada

Manpower in Northern Nevada is currently the only staffing company that covers not only Urban Nevada but all rural Nevada.
Our in-house Risk Management Team handles audits, safety inspections, claim processing, post-accident drug testing, hearings, and OSHA reviews, so we have a competitive edge when it comes to pricing.

Benefits

Reducing turnover and retaining experienced associates means a greater number of proven workers to match your needs. Recognizing the fluid nature of a dependable workforce, the percentage of participation in our benefits program varies widely with market conditions, length of assignments, temp-to-perm hires, and personnel choices.
